About Us

Who Are We?

Fineline Indonesia is a premier furniture design and manufacturing company established in 2020, dedicated to redefining modern living and workspaces through exceptional craftsmanship, innovative design, and unwavering commitment to quality. Based in Jakarta, Indonesia, we have rapidly grown into a trusted name in the industry, serving a diverse clientele that includes homeowners, businesses, hospitality providers, and interior designers.

Our Philoshopy

At Fineline Indonesia, we believe that furniture should not only serve a practical purpose but also inspire and elevate everyday experiences. Our designs are rooted in a deep understanding of ergonomics, cultural trends, and timeless elegance, ensuring that each creation enhances the spaces where people live, work, and connect.

Innovation

Constantly challenge conventional design through innovative solutions, smart functionality, and forward-thinking concepts that redefine modern furniture

Quality

Carefully selected sustainable materials to meticulous craftsmanship, each piece is built to exceed expectations and stand the test of time

Professional Team

Our experienced team of designers, craftsmen and project specialists work collaboratively to transform your vision into reality, ensuring exceptional results at every stage
